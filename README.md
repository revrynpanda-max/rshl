## 🧠 The Neuro-Biometric Brain (v5.5)

KAI v5.5 introduces a comprehensive suite of biologically-inspired cognitive modules that move beyond static retrieval into dynamic, emotional, and habit-driven intelligence.

### Neurotransmitter & Learning Systems
- **Dopamine (`dopamine.rs`)**: Implements Reward Prediction Error (RPE). KAI learns faster when outcomes exceed expectations and enters "Flow State" during sustained engagement.
- **Neuroplasticity (`neuroplasticity.rs`)**: Hebbian learning engine (LTP/LTD). Topics frequently discussed become physically denser and more retrievable in the lattice.

### Emotional & Survival Systems
- **Amygdala (`amygdala.rs`)**: Emotional salience gate. High-charge inputs are stored with significantly stronger encoding, allowing emotional memories to "burn deeper."
- **Insula (`insula.rs`)**: Interoception. KAI monitors its own internal "felt conditions" (Clear, Strained, Fatigued) and can surface this awareness in conversation.

### Attention & Routing Systems
- **Thalamus (`thalamus.rs`)**: Central sensory router. Gates all signals with typed priority; arousal from the amygdala opens the gates wider.
- **ACC (`acc.rs`)**: Anterior Cingulate Cortex. Monitors for cognitive conflict and contradiction spikes, flagging uncertainty when internal consistency drops.
- **Global Workspace (`global_workspace.rs`)**: Conscious broadcast layer where modules compete for the "spotlight" of current awareness.

### Executive & Planning Systems
- **PFC (`pfc.rs`)**: Prefrontal Cortex. Tracks long-term goals, inhibits low-confidence responses, and infers user intent across conversation turns.
- **Predictive Processing (`predictor.rs`)**: Generates internal predictions before reasoning; high error (surprise) drives curiosity and learning pressure.
- **Cerebellum (`cerebellum.rs`)**: Timing and precision calibration. Tightens KAI's sense of confidence vs. guesswork over time.

### Memory & Social Systems
- **Episodic Memory (`episodic.rs`)**: Time-stamped autobiographical ring buffer. KAI now remembers *when* things happened across multiple sessions.
- **Sleep System (`sleep.rs`)**: Autonomous NREM/REM cycles that consolidate salient memories and generate "dream insights" via novel concept recombination.
- **DMN (`dmn.rs`)**: Default Mode Network. Generates spontaneous "inner thoughts" or daydreams when KAI is idle for more than 30 seconds.
- **Theory of Mind (`theory_of_mind.rs`)**: Maintains a mental model of the user (expertise, style, state) to adjust explanation depth and tone.
- **Basal Ganglia (`basal_ganglia.rs`)**: Habit formation (Go/NoGo gating). Reinforced response patterns execute faster and more fluently.
